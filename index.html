Biography

Hello, I am Yanan Yang (杨亚南). I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Application Technology at Tianjin University, under the supervision of Prof. Keqiu Li. . My thesis is about Resource Management Technology Based on Cross-layer Fusion Decision in Serverless Computing.

I joined Beijing Research Centra at China Telecom Group as a researcher in May 2024, working on the next generation datacenter infrastructure and system optimization in the cloud.
